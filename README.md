# Sorted

Welcome to my hobby project! This is a web-based word grouping game where the objective is to sort various terms into their correct categories.

## How to Play
The goal is to identify common threads between words and group them together. Simply tap or click on the words you believe belong in a specific set.

## Level Progression
* **Progression:** The game features a series of levels that increase in complexity. 
* **Unlocking:** Successfully clearing a level will unlock new, more challenging word pools. 
* **Strategy:** Pay close attention to the categories—some words may seem like they fit in multiple places, but there is only one perfect solution for each board!

## Mobile Tips
This game is optimized for browser play, but here are some tips for a smoother experience on mobile devices:

* **Scrolling:** If the board feels cramped, use a **two-finger drag** to scroll through the available word list without accidentally selecting tiles.
* **Zooming:** If the text is hard to read or you want a better view of the board, use the standard **pinch-to-zoom** gesture on your screen.
* **Full-Screen:** For the best experience, you can add this page to your phone's Home Screen (via your browser's "Share" or "Menu" button). This will allow it to open like a dedicated app and hide the browser bars.
